Literacy Program

Literacy for a Lifetime is a 50% grant matching program partnering Usborne Books & More with businesses, foundations, and individuals who support reading at organizations and schools of their choice. Reading is the heart of education! As the leading publisher of childrenâ€™s non-fiction books, Usborne Books & More carries over 1800 Usborne and Kane Miller titles, including fiction, story books, activity books and more, where reading is fun for kids of all ages. The Literacy for a Lifetime program matches any corporate or individual donation or grant that purchases books from Usborne Books & More with an additional 50% in free books. For example, when a corporation donates $10,000 in books from Usborne Books & More to a local elementary school, Usborne Books & More will donate an additional $5,000 in books to the school.

The Literacy for a Lifetime grant matching program is an excellent way to expand a library collection, build a leveled book room, or provide supplemental classroom materials.

The Literacy for a Lifetime program can also be used with the following:

  • State and Federal grants such as:
    • Title I Grants to Local Educational Agencies
    • Improving Literacy through School Libraries
    • Migrant Education
    • Even Start
    • Special Education Preschool Grants
    • Small, Rural School Achievement Program
    • Education for Homeless Children and Youth
    • Twenty-First Century Community Learning Centers
    • Striving Readers
  • Corporate donations or grants
  • Foundation grants
  • PTA/PTSA grants
  • Individual donations
